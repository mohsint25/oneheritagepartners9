Managing Partner

Hassan Tariq is the Co-Founder and Managing Partner at OneHeritage Partners. With over 14 years of experience in finance and technology, Hassan has held leadership roles at premier financial institutions such as JP Morgan, Barclays, and Lazard Asset Management. Most recently, he led global operations for Verition Fund Management.

Previously, Hassan co-founded a food-tech startup recognized by New York City's accelerator program and launched a consulting firm to empower small businesses with AI and cloud strategies. He is pursuing an Executive MBA at Columbia Business School.

Hassan was born in Lahore, Pakistan, and moved to New York City for higher education. Working at local small businesses to fund his college degree, he developed a deep appreciation for small businesses and their contributions to the American economy.

Hassan resides in New York with his wife and two daughters. He loves tennis, motorcycles, and philosophy books and values integrity, innovation, and lifelong learning.

Managing Partner

Mohsin Tariq is a Co-Founder and a Managing Partner at OneHeritage Partners. Prior to OneHeritage Partners, Mohsin was an active operator across various industries such as Logistics, Payments and Consumer Products and led impactful project at DHL, i2cinc and Nestle among others.

Entrepreneur at heart, most recently, Mohsin has been involved in the startup community where he helped launch a first commercial daycare center in a developing country and increase operational efficiency at a travel startup. Previously, he co-founded a cloud kitchen platform recognized by New York City's accelerator program.

He received MBA from the Indiana University Kelley School of Business and Bachelors of Arts from COMSATS University, Pakistan. Born in Lahore, Pakistan, he values legacy, and empowering others.

Mohsin lives in New York with his wife and two children, enjoys staying active through fitness training, playing soccer, and mentoring middle school kids and adults for standardized test success.
